About 428-HI-TECH

428 HI-TECH Group Incorporated is a General Contractor that was founded in 2016 by its President, Karla Mercado.

It's main office is located in Makati city and they have a branch in Coron, Palawan. The main commitment of the company is to provide quality infrastructure and client engage projects. As an industry that offers Design-Build construction, 428 Hi-Tech Group Incorporated provides a full spectrum of pre-construction, program management, design development, construction, interior design management, inspection, and quality control services.
